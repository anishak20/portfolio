Neutron star as a signal for quark-gluon plasma formation

Master's Thesis
Aug 2024- Dec 2025

Description#

Supervisor: Prof. S. Somorendro Singh (Professor, Dept. of Physics and Astrophysics, University of Delhi)

In this study, I conducted an in depth literature review of Quantum Chromodynamics (QCD) focusing on the quark-gluon plasma phase and the MIT Bag Model. I implemented the paper and investigated the Equation of State (EoS) of strange quark matter using the Density Dependent Quark Mass (DDQM) model, an extension of the MIT Bag Model, to better understand the internal structure of neutron stars.
